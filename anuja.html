Anuja Agarwal

About :

Dr. Agarwal specializes in the areas of Innovation, Design Thinking and Innovation Management and New Product Development. She teaches Innovation & Design Thinking in Business Management, Multidisciplinary Course on “Smart cities” , “Fostering Innovation” for the MBA(HR), and “Innovation Management” for Core MBA, Strategic Impact of Emerging Technologies, New Product Development, Human Computer Interaction, Designing Business Interactions, Integrative Manager (Harvard Case Studies on Innovation process), Quantitative Techniques and Research Methodology. She holds a Ph.D. in Management from Mumbai University and an MCA from Devi Ahilya Vishwa Vidyalaya, Indore. She has completed her Bachelors in Computer Science from Delhi University.
